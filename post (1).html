Blog Project
06/18/2024

Now we put it all together in a blog project. I'm excited and itimidated all at the same time. This is our 2nd coding project but the first one felt like a breeze, this is a whole website. Challenge accepted.

So I have ADHD and anxiety which is where the challenge for this project truly lies. I have to be able to plan this project in a way where it seems more manageable for me. At the same time, though, I need to just start because my anxiety might get overwhelmed from mapping out the entire thing, and being able to see how many potential obstacles that I will have to overcome, and then decide we have to put it off (SMH) . So the plan is to start and plan as I go to trick my anxiety and overcome my ADHD symptoms. I won't get overwhelmed (well I will just not as bad) if I plan as I go because I will only plan whatever section I'm currently focused on.

I am very proud of myself for making it this far and attacking these challenges head on. this is something I have always wanted to do, but was too afraid to try it out for myself. I swear perception is everything. I really thought only a specific person could do computer science, and I never thought it would be an African American single mother of 5 children could fit the bill, but I'm glad it's me, maybe I will inspire other moms like me and let them know it's never too late to pursue your dreams. Anyways, wish me luck!
